Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2024

Digital Gift Card Policy

Due to the nature of digital gift cards and instant delivery, we have a strict refund policy:

  • Unused and undelivered gift cards may be eligible for refund
  • Once a gift card code has been revealed or delivered, it cannot be refunded
  • Technical issues preventing code delivery will be eligible for immediate refund

Refund Eligibility

Eligible for Refund:

  • Failed transactions where no code was delivered
  • Double-charged transactions
  • Incorrect product delivery before code revelation

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Revealed or delivered gift card codes
  • Correctly delivered products
  • Changes of mind after code delivery

Refund Process

  1. Contact customer support within 24 hours of purchase
  2. Provide your email address used to purchase and purchase date and reason for refund
  3. Wait for support team verification (usually 1-2 business days)
  4. Receive refund through original payment method

Contact Support

For refund requests or questions, please contact us at refunds@funboxgaming.com
